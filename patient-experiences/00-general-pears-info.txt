WHAT IS ExoVasc PEARS?
----------------------
ExoVasc is a Personalized External Aortic Root Support (PEARS) device. It is a custom-made textile mesh sleeve that is placed around the dilated aorta to support and reinforce it externally.

DURING SURGERY
--------------
- Operation usually takes 2-3 hours
- Done on the BEATING HEART (heart-lung machine NOT normally required)
- Open-chest (sternotomy) surgery

AFTER SURGERY
-------------
- Patients usually spend a few days recovering in hospital
- Most return to normal activity within weeks
- Do NOT require lifelong anticoagulation therapy

COMMUNITY
---------
There is a private Facebook Group called "The Most Exclusive Club on the Planet" for ExoVasc PEARS recipients. Contact Exstent to request access.

This group provides:
- Connection with people of shared experience
- Informed peer support
- Open discussion about living with an aortic condition after surgical treatment
- Does NOT provide medical advice
