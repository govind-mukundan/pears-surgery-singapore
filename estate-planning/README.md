# Estate Planning Pack

This folder contains a practical checklist to help you complete two urgent pre-surgery tasks:

1. Create/update your will.
2. Put a Lasting Power of Attorney (LPA) in place in Singapore.

It is designed for your current asset profile:
- Assets in Singapore and India.
- Assets are financial only (bank savings/fixed deposits and stock market holdings).
- No real estate or other physical assets.

## Suggested Order (Fastest Safe Path)

1. Complete `03-cross-border-asset-checklist.md` first.
2. Complete `01-will-and-testament-sg-india.md` and sign the will.
3. Complete `02-lpa-singapore.md` and submit your LPA.

## Important Notes

- This is planning guidance, not legal advice.
- For cross-border estates, use a Singapore lawyer and an India lawyer to confirm wording and execution formalities.
- Keep original signed documents in a fire-safe folder and tell your executor where they are kept.
