Active Global Caregivers — activeglobalcaregiver.sg
This is probably your best fit. Their caregivers are foreign-trained nurses, directly selected by senior nurses, and hospitals refer complex cases to them.  They have cardiac-experienced nurses on their roster, including caregivers with intensive coronary care unit and medical ICU experience.  All new caregivers are retrained at their Caregiver Training Centre at Goldhill Plaza before starting work, and nurse-trainers conduct home visits to review your care plan.  Salary from SGD $600–$1,000/month. 
Anglo Caregivers — anglocaregivers.com
Caregivers can join you in as little as 2 weeks, speak English or Malay, and have experience with post-surgical patients. Basic salary from $650–$800/month.  Good option if you want to move quickly.
Top Faith Caregiver — topfaithcaregiver.com
A nurse-founded platform for certified caregivers from Myanmar, Philippines and Indonesia.  Worth getting a quote from to compare.
Xedge Resource — xedgeresource.com
Specializes in placement of experienced live-in caregivers and will match caregivers with appropriate skill sets to your specific needs. 
Retire Genie — retiregenie.com
Offers live-in caregivers for 24/7 monitoring, and can also arrange a private interview between you and suitable applicants so you can assess personality and fit before committing.
