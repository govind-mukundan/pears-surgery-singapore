Hello,

I am writing to inquire about your live-in caregiver services. I will be undergoing elective open-heart surgery in a few months and am looking for post-operative recovery support at home.

I live alone and have a spare bedroom available for the caregiver. My budget is flexible for the right care setup.

**My Requirements:**
1. **Duration:** Short-term contract for 1 to 2 months, depending on my recovery speed.
2. **Medical Needs:** Assistance with daily activities (showering, mobility) and monitoring for post-surgical complications. I require someone with basic medical familiarity who can assist me in booking a cab to the hospital in an emergency.
3. **Household Duties:** Cooking healthy, heart-friendly meals and light household cleaning.
4. **Pet Situation (Crucial):** I have two cats. I understand that caregivers may not administer injections, but one of my cats requires insulin shots. I (or a friend) will provide full training. Please let me know:
    - Will the caregiver be permitted to administer the insulin after being trained?

Could you please provide:
1. A quote for a 1-month and 2-month short-term live-in arrangement.
2. The lead time required to secure a caregiver.
3. Confirmation on the pet care restrictions mentioned above.

Thank you,
